About Problem Bank

Problem Bank is a free practice tool for British GCSE and A-Level students studying Maths and Physics. Every problem is randomly generated, so you can practise the same topic as many times as you need without ever seeing the same question twice.

How to Use It

  1. Select your level (GCSE or A-Level)
  2. Choose a subject and topic
  3. Pick Revision mode to choose your difficulty, or Exam mode for a realistic mix
  4. Click Generate Problem and attempt it before revealing the answer
  5. Use the 💡 key concept hint if you need a nudge in the right direction
