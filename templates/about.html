About Problem Bank

Free revision and practice for GCSE, A-Level, and IB MYP — maths, physics, and computer science.

Revision notes Generated questions Instant answers

Problem Bank helps you revise a topic, then practise with fresh exam-style questions. Content is aimed at UK exam boards; many topics also suit general study and revision.

What you can do
  • Browse topics — open a subject area for explanations, worked examples, and quick checks.
  • Generate a question — pick a level, subject, topic, and difficulty to get a new problem with a full solution.
  • Quick test — after generating a question, start a short run of 10 questions on the same settings.
  • Lesson quizzes — on some topic pages, try a short multiple-choice quiz to check what you remember.
Practice generator options

On the generator you can choose:

  • Level — GCSE, A-Level, or IB MYP
  • Subject — Mathematics, Physics, or Computer Science (availability depends on level)
  • Topic — a specific area within that subject
  • Mode — standard written questions, or multiple choice
  • Difficulty — foundational, intermediate, or difficult

Each question includes a worked answer. Many also include a hint if you are stuck.

Coverage

The site is growing. At the moment you will find, among others:

  • GCSE Maths — algebra, geometry, number, statistics, trigonometry, and more
  • GCSE Physics — forces, radioactivity, and related topics
  • GCSE Computer Science — Python, algorithms, data representation, networks, cyber security, and other theory topics
  • A-Level Physics — fields, particles, photoelectric effect, and related areas
  • IB MYP Chemistry — selected units such as redox and energy changes

See the full list on the Topics page.

Accounts

You can use the generator without signing in. A free account lets you unlock difficult questions, save questions, bookmark your place in lessons, and track lesson quiz scores.

You must be 13 or older to register. By signing up you agree to use Problem Bank for personal study. We store your email and handle so you can log in; we do not sell your data.
